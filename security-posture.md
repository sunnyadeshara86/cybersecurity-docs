# Security posture

Over the years, security investments have transitioned from being optional to essential. Today, organizations worldwide recognize the critical importance of consistently investing in security measures. Such investment is crucial for maintaining a competitive edge in the market. Neglecting to adequately protect their assets can result in irreparable harm and, in some cases, lead to bankruptcy. Given the current threat landscape, merely investing in protection is insufficient. Organizations must improve their overall security posture, ensuring that investments in protection, detection, and response are well-coordinated.
